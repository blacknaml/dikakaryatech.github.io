SIDIK — Hospital Training Management System

In a competitive healthcare environment, the quality of your services depends on the competence of your staff. SIDIK is a web-based training management system designed to help hospitals plan, deliver, and track staff development programs — efficiently and fairly.

From scheduling training sessions to managing certifications, SIDIK provides a complete solution for hospital staff development.

  • Training Program Management

    Design and manage training schedules with ease.

  • Participant Tracking

    Monitor registration, attendance, and progress in real time.

  • Interactive Learning Materials

    Share engaging content for more effective training.

  • Automated Certification

    Generate and manage training certificates quickly.

  • Web-Based

    Accessible from any browser — desktop or mobile.

  • Public Landing Page

    A public-facing page for general information about your training department.

  • Covers 3 categories

    Diklit (internal employee training), Kordik (internship & external institution programs), KEP (external research management).

SIMAPRO — Project Management Information System

Maximize your project efficiency. SIMAPRO is an integrated web-based software solution that helps organizations plan, manage, and monitor every aspect of their projects — from initial planning to budget management — giving you full control to ensure delivery on time and on target.

SIMAPRO uses a multi-level approval system with role-based access for Admin, Directors, Purchasing, Accounting, and Project Managers.

  • Web-Based

    Accessible from any browser on desktop or mobile.

  • Multi-Level Approval

    Structured workflow with role-based access control.

  • PIECES Analysis

    Built-in analysis covering Performance, Information, Economy, Control, Efficiency, and Service indicators.

  • Budget Management

    Track project costs and spending in real time.
